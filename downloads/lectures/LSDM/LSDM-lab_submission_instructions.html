Please find below the instructions for submitting your work for the labs of the course Data Management in Large Scale Distributed Systems.

Important information

  • Submission deadline: January 21, 2019
  • Only one graded lab should be submitted
  • Labs are to be done by groups of at most 2 students

Submission process

  • Submit your lab by email at the address lsdm.grenoble@gmail.com
  • Each group should submit only once
  • Your email should include a single archive containing your source code for the lab and your report
    • We recall that your report should be submitted either in md (MarkDown) or pdf format
  • The body of your email should include
    • The name of the participants
    • The name of the lab on which you have worked (Lab Spark or Lab Performance)
